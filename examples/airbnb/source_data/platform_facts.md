# Airbnb Facts & Figures — Source Research

Companion to the Airbnb policy pack. This file holds **verifiable numbers and concrete
facts** (fees, refund windows, coverage amounts, times) needed to ground Truth Check,
Search Quality, and Compare Versions examples. Every fact cites an official Airbnb Help
Center URL.

- Research date: July 16, 2026
- Primary source domain: airbnb.com (Help Center)
- Scope: factual figures only; policy/prohibition rules live in the separate policy pack.

---

## 1. Service fees

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/1857

- **Guest service fee:** ranges from **14.1% to 16.5%** of the booking subtotal. The
  subtotal is the nightly price plus host-charged fees, excluding the guest service fee
  and taxes.
- **Cross-currency bookings:** guest service fee can reach **up to 16.5%** of the subtotal.
- **Host service fee — split-fee structure:** most hosts pay **3%**; Brazil and Mexico
  listings pay **4%**. Automatically deducted from the payout.
- **Host service fee — host-only (single-fee) structure:** most hosts pay **15.5%**;
  remaining hosts typically pay **14%–16%**; Brazil and Mexico listings pay **16%**.
  Entire fee comes from the host payout.
- **Services:** typically a **15%** fee, minimum **$6 USD**. **Experiences:** typically **20%**.
  (Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3164)
- **Refundability:** the guest service fee is **refundable for home stays** when all
  cancellation criteria are met.
- **Tax:** the service fee shown to hosts and guests is **VAT-inclusive where applicable**.

## 2. Guest cancellation policies (host-selectable tiers)

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/475

- **Flexible:** full refund (incl. taxes) if the guest cancels **up to 24 hours before
  check-in**.
- **Moderate:** full refund (incl. taxes) if the guest cancels **up to 5 days before
  check-in**.
- **Firm:** full refund if canceled **at least 30 days before check-in**. Cancel
  **7–30 days** before → host paid **50%**. Cancel **less than 7 days** before → host
  paid **100%**.
- **Strict:** full refund only if canceled **within 48 hours of booking AND at least 28
  days before check-in**. After that window → host paid **100%** of nights.
- **Limited (newer):** full refund (incl. taxes) if canceled **up to 14 days before
  check-in**.

## 3. Host cancellation penalties

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/990

- Cancellation fee is a **% of the reservation amount for nights not stayed**, with a
  **minimum fee of $50 USD**:
  - **48 hours or less** before check-in → **50%**
  - **48 hours to 30 days** before check-in → **25%**
  - **more than 30 days** before check-in → **10%**
- The **reservation amount** for the fee = base rate + cleaning fee + pet fees;
  **excludes** taxes and guest fees.
- Additional consequences: the affected **dates are blocked** on the calendar; host may
  have listing/account **suspended or removed** and may **lose Superhost status**; no
  payout for the canceled reservation.

## 4. AirCover for guests

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3218

- Included **free with every home booking**.
- Covers: host **cancels before check-in**; guest **can't contact the host**; listing is
  **significantly different than advertised** and the host can't resolve it (e.g. fewer
  bedrooms than listed, private room instead of whole home, missing major advertised
  amenity like a pool or kitchen, heating not working in winter).
- Does **not** cover minor inconveniences (e.g. a broken toaster) or travel-related issues.
- **Reporting window:** notify the host **within 72 hours of discovery**.
- Remedy: Airbnb helps rebook a comparable place at comparable pricing; if none is
  available or the guest declines, a **full or partial refund including service fees**.
- **Not an insurance policy.** Includes a 24-hour safety line (phone, email, chat).

## 5. AirCover for Hosts

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3142

- **$3M USD Host damage protection** — guest-caused damage to home and belongings, plus
  specialized cleaning (stains, pet accidents, smoke odor).
- **$1M USD Host liability insurance** — guest injury or their property damage/theft
  during a stay.
- **$1M USD Experiences & Services liability insurance** — guest injury during
  Experiences or Services.
- Also includes guest identity verification, reservation screening, and a 24-hour safety
  line.
- Regional exclusions: does not apply to Airbnb Travel, LLC stays or hosts in Japan
  (separate insurance programs).

## 6. Booking, check-in & checkout

- **Default times:** if the host sets none, defaults are **3:00 PM check-in** and
  **11:00 AM checkout** (listing local time). Hosts can change these.
  (Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/1296)
- **Self check-in:** door/lockbox code is emailed and messaged **48 hours before arrival**.
  (Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/1207)
- **Early check-in / late checkout** can be requested but are host-approved.
  (Sources: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3431 , https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3433)

## 7. Reviews

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/995

- Both parties have **14 days after checkout** to submit a review; the window starts on
  the morning of checkout.
- Reviews are published **once both are submitted or the 14-day period ends** (whichever
  comes first).
- Reservations **canceled before the day of check-in** cannot be reviewed; ones canceled
  **on/after the day of check-in** (12:00 AM listing time) may be reviewable.
- You can **edit** your review within the 14-day window until the other party submits.

## 8. Security cameras & privacy devices

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3061

- **Indoor cameras/recording devices are banned** — not allowed to monitor any indoor
  space (bedrooms, bathrooms, hallways, living rooms), **even if turned off**.
- **Effective date: April 30, 2024.**
- **Outdoor cameras allowed but must be disclosed**, with a description and location
  (e.g. "camera in my front yard"). Not allowed over areas with a high expectation of
  privacy (enclosed outdoor shower, sauna).
- **Noise decibel monitors** (measure sound level/duration, don't record audio) allowed
  indoors **if disclosed** and **not in bedrooms, bathrooms, or sleeping areas**.

## 9. Major Disruptive Events Policy (formerly Extenuating Circumstances)

Sources: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/1320 , https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/2930

- Covers cancellations from major events (natural disasters, government travel
  restrictions, declared public health emergencies/epidemics) that occur **after
  booking**, affect the reservation location, and prevent/legally prohibit completion.
- When it applies, guests can cancel for a **refund, travel credit, and/or other
  consideration regardless of the listing's cancellation policy**, and hosts can cancel
  **without fees or adverse consequences**.
- **Exclusions:** **COVID-19 is not covered**; foreseeable/common weather or natural
  conditions are excluded **unless** they cause another covered event (e.g. government
  restriction, large-scale utility outage).
- Revised policy applies to trips/experiences on or after **June 6, 2024**, regardless of
  when booked.

## 10. Age requirements

Source: https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/2876 , https://www.airbnb.com/help/article/3930

- You must be **at least 18** to create an account to travel or host (Terms of Service).
- In the US and other specified countries, hosts may set a booking-guest age minimum
  **up to 25**, where permitted by law.
